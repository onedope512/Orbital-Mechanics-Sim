Placing Bodies

Select a preset from the grid on the left (Earth, Sun, Black Hole, etc.) or leave it blank for a custom body. Adjust Mass, Size, and Color, then click + PLACE or click anywhere on the simulation canvas. Without a preset selected, bodies are named Body 1, Body 2, etc.

Velocity Arrow

When placing, click and drag on the canvas to set an initial velocity. The longer the drag, the faster the launch. Release to place the body.

Top Bar Controls

⏸ / ▶ Pause/Play Freeze or resume the simulation.
⏩ Speed slider Simulation speed multiplier. A warning appears above 50×.
🔁 Reverse Run physics backwards in time.
🎯 Lock On Locks the camera to follow the selected body. Press Esc or click Unlock to release.
💾 Save / 📂 Load Export or import the full scenario as a JSON file.

Keyboard Shortcuts

C center on origin
Esc deselect / exit mode
? toggle this help

Orbit Controls

Left drag Rotate the camera around the target point.
Right drag Pan the camera (shift the target point).
Scroll wheel Zoom in/out toward the cursor position — the view re-centres on wherever you scroll.

Camera Modes

⊕ Center on Origin Snap the camera back to (0, 0, 0). Also press C.
🎯 Lock On Select a body in the list, then click Lock On in the top bar. Camera follows that body continuously.
Lock Camera to CoM Camera follows the system's centre of mass. Useful for multi-body systems.
Follow (per-body) Click the green FOL button next to any body in the list.

Tips

Zoom-to-cursor means you can zoom into any body without manually panning first — just hover over it and scroll.

Body List (right panel)

Every placed body appears in the list on the right. Click a name to select it. Shift+click to multi-select. Double-click a name to rename it.

🚀 button Select body and enable WASD thrust control.
👁 button Lock camera to follow that body.
👻 button Toggle Ghost mode — body passes through everything (no collisions).
✕ button Remove the body from the simulation.

Selected Body Controls

Selecting a body expands it to show Mass and Size adjust buttons (−/+). Mass adjusts by ±15% per click. Size adjusts the visual radius by ±2 units.

Multi-Select

◎ CoM Lock Camera follows the centre of mass of just your selected group.
🚀 Group THR WASD thrust applies to all selected bodies simultaneously.
Delete All Remove all selected bodies at once.

Presets

The 12 preset tiles (Asteroid to Black Hole) set mass, radius, colour, and density tag automatically. You can override Mass and Size before placing. The preset name becomes the body's default label.

Collisions

When two bodies overlap they merge — momentum is fully conserved. The merged body inherits the heavier body's name with the smaller appended (e.g. Sun+Earth). Enable Ghost mode to prevent collisions.

Integrator

The simulation uses a symplectic leapfrog (Velocity-Verlet) integrator — this conserves energy over long periods so orbits stay stable indefinitely. Euler or RK4 would slowly gain or lose energy.

Thrust Mode (WASD)

Select a body, click THR next to it, and use keyboard controls to apply thrust each physics step:

W / S accelerate / brake
A / D strafe left / right
Q / E up / down

Presets — Orbital Scenarios

Binary Stars Two equal-mass stars in a stable mutual orbit.
Solar System Sun + scaled rocky and gas planets in circular orbits.
Figure-8 Three equal-mass bodies in the stable figure-8 choreography.
Hohmann Transfer A ship executes a two-burn transfer from an inner to outer orbit.
Lagrange Points Sun + Jupiter + trojan asteroids at L4/L5 stable points.

Energy Display

The overlay (bottom-right) shows body count, centre of mass, FPS, and energy drift percentage. For stable orbits, drift should stay near 0%.

Gravitational Well Grid

The simulation grid is also a live gravitational potential surface — it deforms downward under massive bodies. A black hole creates a sharp spike. Use the sliders to adjust:

Well Detail How far from a body the grid deforms. Higher = larger wells, more GPU work.
Grid Brightness Opacity/brightness of all grid lines (goes above 100% to fully saturate).
Grid Frequency Spacing of grid lines — slide right for a finer, denser grid.

Vectors & Labels

Vectors Toggle velocity (cyan) and acceleration (orange) arrows on all bodies.
Labels Toggle floating name labels above each body.
Trails Toggle orbital path trails. Trail length is controlled by the Trail Length slider.
Grav Well Toggle the grid displacement effect on/off.

Scenario Recorder

Click ⏺ Record to start capturing frames, ⏹ Stop to finish. Use the playback slider to scrub through the recording or press ▶ Play to replay.
